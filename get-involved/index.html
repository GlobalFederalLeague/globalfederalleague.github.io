GET INVOLVED

MAKE AN ARTISTIC OR INTELLECTUAL DONATION

Our organization highly appreciates the use of art, music, dance and literature as a means to spread peace and harmony. We would always be open to your kind contributions for our cause. A genuine expressive painting or a deep thought provoking musical composition would be as helpful for us as a handsome financial grant. Currently we are a zero dollar organization and would be remaining so in the near future, hence your artistic and intellectual contributions would be our sole capital.

COLLABORATE WITH US

Our organization is ever enthusiastic about collaborating with other similar volunteer organizations and with the organizers of non profit events such as debates quizzes, MUNs and so on. We would be more than happy to provide a brief presentation at your opening and closing ceremonies, without any remuneration.

VOLUNTEER AS A TEMPORARY MEMBER

This is one of the simplest ways to help out our cause. We believe the best way for our initiatives to be successful is for the community to actively get involved. This is an easy and efficient way of contributing to the great work we do at Global Federal League. Get in touch with any questions about how you can Volunteer Your Time today. From being a consultant to sharing our message among your contacts, there are numerous ways in which you can volunteer. The Organisation would issue an official digital badge commemorating your valuable contributions.
