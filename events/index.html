Garry Davis' Centennial Anniversary

As a pioneer of the World Federalist movement and a persevering gadfly, Garry Davis was no armchair theorist and perhaps the only cosmocrat who completely devoted his entire life to the cause. From renouncing his American Citizenship to storming into the UN, from issuing world passports and documents to crossing borders on the same- Garry's life was a rollercoaster ride.

Though Garry doesn't feature in history textbooks, he stays in hearts of passionate World Federalists. On his hundredth birth anniversary, we pledge to uphold his struggle and keep it alive against the test of time.
